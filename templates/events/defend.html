Defend and Dominate

About the Event

A dynamic debate event testing quick thinking, adaptability, and communication by switching between "Tackle" and "Block" perspectives.

Event Details

  • Round 1: Quick Thinking – Participants have 3 minutes to present their stance, showcasing adaptability, logic, and clarity.
  • Round 2: Strategy and Depth – Qualified participants get 5 minutes to explore their topic, with increased command frequency to test fluency, creativity, and endurance.

Rules

  • Participants compete individually, with topics and commands assigned by the moderator.
  • The moderator may call "Block" or "Tackle" anytime, requiring instant argument shifts.
  • Judging is based on adaptability, creativity, and clarity; only top scorers move to Round 2.
  • All arguments must be respectful and relevant to the topic.
