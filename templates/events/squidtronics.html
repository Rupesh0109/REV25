About the Event

Squidtronics is an intense three-round competition where teams battle through electronics-themed challenges, with eliminations at each stage to determine the ultimate winner.

Event Details

  • Round 1 - Riddle the Circuit: A quiz round where teams solve electronics-related riddles using a buzzer system; low-scoring teams are eliminated.
  • Round 2 - Circuit Relay: Teams collaboratively draw a circuit diagram without verbal communication; the first six teams to complete it qualify for the final round.
  • Round 3 - Circuit Showdown: The top six teams compete in three tasks—technical quiz, coding challenge, and soldering a circuit—with the fastest and most accurate team winning

Rules

  • Participants must follow event rules and avoid any form of malpractice; violations may lead to disqualification.
  • Each event must be completed within the allotted time
  • Teams will be eliminated at each stage based on performance, with only the top competitors advancing.
  • Participants must arrive on time, follow instructions, and maintain discipline throughout the event
