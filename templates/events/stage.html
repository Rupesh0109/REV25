Stage Unplugged

About the Event

An event where participants can showcase their talent in singing, dancing, acting, poetry, comedy, or storytelling in any style or genre.

Event Details

  • Participants can choose to perform in singing, dancing, or acting & performance categories.
  • Performances can be solo, duet, or group, with diverse genres and styles welcomed.
  • Each performance must adhere to the designated time limit to ensure fair participation.
  • Background music or necessary props must be submitted in advance if required.

Rules

  • Performances can be in English or Tamil and must be 2 to 5 minutes long.
  • Background music, if needed, must be submitted in advance.
  • Content must be respectful, avoiding offensive language or gestures.
  • Judging is based on creativity, presence, expression, execution, and impact; decisions are final.
