Treasure Hunt

About the Event

The Treasure Hunt is an exciting, clue-based challenge that tests participants' problem-solving skills and teamwork as they race to uncover hidden treasures

Event Details

  • Abstract Submission - Participants register online and submit their research abstract via Google Forms
  • Shortlisting: A panel of judges reviews abstracts and shortlists participants based on quality and relevance
  • Paper Submission & Payment: Shortlisted teams submit full research papers and pay the event registration fee.
  • Participants present their research to a jury and audience, followed by a Q&A session.

Rules

  • Maximum 2-3 members in each team.
  • Teams solving tasks in the shortest time will be given priority.
  • Venue details will be shared before the event begins.
  • Teams must follow the instructions given at each stage to avoid disqualification.
